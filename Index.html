Strategic Shift to a Hybrid Model

This section outlines the decision to adopt a conversation-first support model, the key benefits driving this change, and how it evolves from our current system.

The Decision

Conversation-first with auto-conversion for defined teams.

Why this change?

  • ‚úì Faster for simple conversations
  • ‚úì Accountable for complex issues
  • ‚úì Clear customer visibility via Ticket ID and state
  • ‚úì Better data to improve roadmap and ops

Current Model at Tibber

Our existing process serves as the foundation for this evolution.

  • Live chat starts as a Conversation in LiveChat teams.
  • Auto-converted to a Ticket when assigned to defined team inboxes.
  • Manually converted when specific complexity criteria are met.

How The New Process Works

Explore the detailed mechanics of the new hybrid model, from the initial conversation to ticket management and team collaboration.

Decision Tree: When to Convert

All Interactions Start as a Conversation

Convert to a Ticket when:

  • Issue spans multiple teams or >1 workday
  • Compliance, billing, or engineering are involved
  • Customer requests a formal status update
  • It's a bug or a feature request

Workflow & Automation

  • Assignment to Defined Team-Inboxes triggers conversion to Ticket.
  • System sets Type & SLA, adds watchers, notifies Slack, and sends customer an acknowledgement with Ticket ID.
  • Ticket state triggers manage the lifecycle and handle auto-closure.
